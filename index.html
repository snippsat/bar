The Craft

From Bean
to Cup

A meticulous journey of transformation — where science meets intuition, and patience yields perfection.

Step 01

Sourcing

We travel directly to origin, tasting hundreds of lots to select only the top 2% of each harvest. Every bean is chosen for its unique character and story.

Step 02

Roasting

Our master roasters coax out each bean's potential through precise temperature curves — developed over decades and refined with every batch.

Step 03

Cupping

Every roast is rigorously cupped and scored. We evaluate aroma, acidity, body, and finish — ensuring only exceptional coffee bears the Noire name.

Step 04

Delivery

Sealed at peak freshness and shipped within 24 hours of roasting. Because extraordinary coffee deserves to arrive exactly as intended.
